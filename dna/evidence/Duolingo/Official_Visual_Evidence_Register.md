# Duolingo Official Visual Evidence Register

Retrieved: 2026-06-19

This register contains only measurable visual facts from Duolingo's official brand-guidelines product and its published first-party stylesheet. It is the evidence set used by `dna/visual/DuolingoVisualDNA/DuolingoVisualDNA_v1.md`. No blogs, reviews, forums, AI-generated content, or personal opinion is included.

## EVID-001 — Official core and secondary colors

- Official source: https://design.duolingo.com/identity/color#core-brand-colors
- Official source: https://design.duolingo.com/identity/color#secondary-colors
- Observed evidence: Duolingo names Feather Green `#58CC02` as its core brand color, Mask Green `#89E219` as its secondary green, Eel `#4B4B4B` as a typography color, and Snow `#FFFFFF` as its primary background color. The official secondary palette includes Macaw `#1CB0F6`, Cardinal `#FF4B4B`, Bee `#FFC800`, Fox `#FF9600`, Beetle `#CE82FF`, and Humpback `#2B70C9`. The official neutral palette includes Polar `#F7F7F7`, Swan `#E5E5E5`, Hare `#AFAFAF`, Wolf `#777777`, Eel `#4B4B4B`, and Snow `#FFFFFF`.

## EVID-002 — Official typography

- Official source: https://design.duolingo.com/identity/typography
- Observed evidence: Feather Bold is the official typeface for large, brief headlines, left-aligned lists, and sentences ending with Duolingo; it uses weight 700, lowercase, and 100–110% leading. DIN Next Rounded is the official typeface for headlines longer than 10 words, subheadings, and body copy; it uses 140% leading. When the two faces appear together, Feather Bold is 150% larger, DIN Next Rounded must not be larger, and the faces must not be combined within one sentence. Nunito is the documented substitute font. The published site CSS supplies DIN Round weights 300, 400, 500, and 700; Feather weight 700; and Nunito weights 300, 400, 600, and 700.

- Official source: https://design.duolingo.com/
- Official stylesheet: https://design.duolingo.com/main.9d8e47afeb08c26c0f6b.css

It publishes spacing utilities for `8`, `12`, `16`, `20`, `24`, `30`, `32`, `40`, `50`, and `70px`, grid gaps including `10`, `15`, `16`, `20`, `24`, `30`, `40`, and `50px`, and column gaps including `24`, `30`, `40`, `50`, `60`, and `70px`.

## EVID-004 — Official component styling

- Official source: https://design.duolingo.com/
- Official stylesheet: https://design.duolingo.com/main.9d8e47afeb08c26c0f6b.css
- Observed evidence: The official stylesheet defines bordered cards with Snow backgrounds, `2px` Swan borders, `10px` corner radii, and `0 2px 0` Swan shadows. It defines a larger content tile with Polar background and `8px` corners. Guide cards use `16px` corners, white text, and a hover brightness of `1.1`. Dividers use `2px` Swan borders. Navigation panels use `2px` Swan borders, `12px` corners, and responsive widths of `180`, `200`, `220`, and `240px`. Uppercase labels use weight 700 and letter spacing of `0.5–0.75px`.

## EVID-005 — Official visual motion

- Official source: https://design.duolingo.com/
- Official stylesheet: https://design.duolingo.com/main.9d8e47afeb08c26c0f6b.css
- Observed evidence: The official stylesheet uses `300ms` transitions for opacity, color, transforms, filter, and menu-line state changes with the default CSS timing function (`ease`). Carousel/content translations use `800ms` transform transitions. The off-canvas panel begins at `translateX(-100%)` and enters at `transform: none`; its overlay transitions opacity over `300ms` to `0.3`. Expand indicators rotate `90deg` over `300ms`. Guide-card hover changes brightness to `1.1` over `300ms`.
